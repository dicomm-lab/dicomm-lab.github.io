Deep and Intelligent Communications Laboratory

Our research philosophy focuses on exploring in-depth and conducting multidimensional analyses of challenges across diverse communication systems. We aim to address these challenges through the application of innovative, intelligent, and multidisciplinary approaches to deliver effective and optimized solutions.

우리 연구실은 다양한 통신 시스템에서 발생하는 문제를 깊이 있게 탐구하고 다각적으로 분석하는 것을 중심으로 연구를 진행합니다. 이러한 문제를 해결하기 위해 스마트하고 혁신적인 공학적 방법론과 다학제적 접근을 활용하여 최적화된 솔루션을 제시하는 것을 목표로 합니다.

My work involves utilizing a variety of mathematical tools, including matrix theory, large-scale optimization, sampling, information and communication theory, digital signal processing, and stochastic geometry. My objective is to develop effective algorithms and analyze wireless systems, with a focus on enhancing efficiency.

Some of my current research topics are:

  • Wireless Communications
  • Signal Processing
  • Sensing Technologies
  • Machine Learning Applications
  • Optimization Algorithms
